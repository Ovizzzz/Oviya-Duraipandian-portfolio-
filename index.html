FI–MM Integration

Why it matters

When a Goods Receipt posts in MM (MIGO), SAP automatically generates FI entries — debiting Stock and crediting GR/IR. When the invoice arrives (MIRO), the GR/IR clears against the vendor account. I can diagnose mismatches because I understand this logic at its root.

Procure-to-Pay Cycle

End-to-end ownership

P2P is a chain of business events where any broken link causes payment delays or audit findings. Having operated the full cycle in a live retail environment means I understand the urgency of each step and the downstream impact of errors.

Case Studies

Real Problems. Real Solutions.

01
SAP MM/FI · Sephora · Paris
GR/IR Account Discrepancy Blocking Month-End Close
Context

A GR/IR clearing account showed an uncleared balance preventing the finance team from completing month-end reconciliation on time.

My Approach

Investigated the GR/IR report to identify POs with goods receipts but no matching invoices. Cross-referenced PO history in ME21N and traced goods movement documents — identifying a GR posted against a cancelled PO line item.

Result

Escalated with full documentation. Month-end close completed on schedule. Ticket closed within SLA.

02
SAP FI · EID Parry · Chennai
Vendor Master Data Inconsistency Causing Payment Errors
Context

Several vendor payments flagged errors due to inconsistent bank details across duplicate vendor master records.

My Approach

Reviewed 30+ vendor master records, comparing general data and company code data. Identified records with missing or mismatched bank details and updated under the approval workflow.

Result

Cleared the payment error backlog. Improved master data quality, reducing future reconciliation exceptions.

03
SAP MM · Corefa Integration
External PO Sync Failure Between Corefa and SAP
Context

A batch of external POs processed via Corefa failed to sync into SAP, causing missing PO records and procurement delays.

My Approach

Investigated FileZilla transfer logs to confirm file delivery, then checked SAP inbound processing queue. Identified a field format mismatch in the PO data file as root cause.

Result

Escalated with diagnostic evidence. Proposed a validation check step to prevent recurrence.
